Hello friends!

My name is Lydia and I am 31 years old. I started this blog to share some of the most stunning places I've had the honor of visiting and living in. I was born in Seattle, WA, and lived there until I was 24. Shortly after marrying my husband, Ryan, we moved to England, where we spent three wonderful years. England is rich in people, historic buildings, castles, wildlife, and breathtaking scenery. During our time there, we explored various parts of the UK, including Wales, Scotland, and the Scottish Highlands. We also traveled to Germany and Liechtenstein before the Covid-19 pandemic brought everything to a halt.
During my time in England, I had the opportunity to work. My first job was as a barista at a small shop called Little Breads. I had no experience with an espresso machine, but I was fortunate to have an excellent teacher, Bradley! After about 8 months, Covid-19 hit, and I found myself unable to work for nearly a year.
